Welcome to the Savannah Exhibit

Step into the African Savannah and encounter a diverse range of wildlife in their natural habitat. From majestic lions to graceful giraffes, this exhibit offers a unique opportunity to connect with the untamed beauty of the African plains.

Featured Animals

Our exhibit is home to a variety of incredible creatures that call the Savannah home. You can observe these animals up close and learn about their behaviors, conservation efforts, and the importance of preserving their natural environment.

Cheetah

Welcome to our zoo's Cheetah Exhibit, where you can get up close and personal with one of the most awe-inspiring creatures of the wild, the cheetah (Acinonyx jubatus). Known as the "Speed Demon of the Savannah," the cheetah is a marvel of nature's engineering.

Gazelle

Welcome to our zoo's Gazelle Habitat, where you can experience the beauty and elegance of one of Africa's most captivating creatures, the gazelle. Step into the world of these graceful antelopes and witness their stunning agility and adaptability up close.
