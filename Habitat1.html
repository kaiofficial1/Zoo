Welcome to the Antarctic Exhibit

Discover the wonders of the Antarctic at our zoo's incredible exhibit. Experience the harsh beauty of this frozen continent and get up close and personal with some of its most fascinating inhabitants.

Featured Animals

Our exhibit is home to a variety of amazing creatures that have adapted to survive in the extreme conditions of Antarctica. From playful penguins to majestic seals, you'll have the opportunity to observe these incredible animals in a habitat designed to mimic their natural environment.

Emperor Penguin

Welcome to our zoo's Emperor Penguin Exhibit, where you can dive into the enchanting world of the largest and most iconic penguin species on Earth, the Emperor penguin (Aptenodytes forsteri). Step into their icy realm and discover the remarkable lives of these regal birds.

Polar Bear

Welcome to our zoo's Polar Bear Habitat, where you can embark on a thrilling journey to the frozen wilderness of the Arctic and come face to face with the magnificent polar bear (Ursus maritimus). Step into a world of ice and wonder as you learn about these iconic and awe-inspiring creatures.
