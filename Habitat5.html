Welcome to the Sahara Exhibit

Welcome to our Sahara Desert Exhibit, an immersive experience that transports you to the heart of one of the world's most iconic and challenging landscapes. Step into a world of endless dunes, fascinating wildlife, and the timeless beauty of the Sahara.

Featured Animals

Our exhibit is home to a variety of incredible creatures that call the Savannah home. You can observe these animals up close and learn about their behaviors, conservation efforts, and the importance of preserving their natural environment.

Fennec Fox

Welcome to our Fennec Fox Exhibit, a place where you can get up close and personal with one of the desert's most enchanting and diminutive creatures. Step into the world of the Fennec fox (Vulpes zerda), a captivating and elusive inhabitant of arid landscapes.
